Online Meeting Service Positions

Host

  • Start meeting 15 minutes early for fellowship
  • Mute all speakers promptly at meeting start time
  • Mute wacamole during meeting
  • Lower speaker's hand after share
  • Be ready to stop user from sharing
  • Be ready to mute user video if innappropriate content (eg vaping)
  • Be ready to remove a user if they are disruptive or offensive
  • Rename the Secretary on Zoom if they forget: "First L -- LGHG"

Reading Displayer

  • Display meeting readings as needed
  • Display Book Study readings if necessary
  • Show Serenity Prayer, Third Step Prayer, and "Keep Coming Back"

Timer

  • Share audio only after first speaker
  • Play chime sound when secretary pauses for chime
  • Time each speaker using stopwatch
  • Play first chime 20 seconds before their time is up
  • Repeatedly play chimes every 20 seconds until they quit

Chat Moderator

  • Watch the chat stream for messages that need responding to
  • Display chat announcements from Chat Announcements
